Where did it start?

If you notice, on this page there is a beautiful couple enjoying the few summer months in Rio Grande do Sul (the southernmost state in Brazil), well, these are my parents in the early 70s.
After a few years of marriage, they had my brother (1981) and me (1989) and the rest is coming.
Below is also the location of the house where I grew up in Estrela/RS, a small town in the interior of my state, with just over 30,000 inhabitants, typically German colonized (I even have records that my great-grandparents arrived in Brazil between 1890 and 1907).
